Gabrielle-Ann Torre

Hello! I am a PhD candidate studying human cognitive neuroscience. How do our IQ and socioeconomic status (SES) interactively and individually impact brain structure throughout development? How do these relationships shape reading and math abilities? I use various methods in structural neuroimaging--voxel-based morphometry (VBM), cortical thickness, and diffusion tensor imaging (DTI)--to tackle these questions.

I am currently a student at Georgetown University in the lab of Guinevere Eden at the Center for the Study of Learning, where we conduct studies on the neural basis of typical and atypical development.
